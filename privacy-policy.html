Privacy, Simplified

No legalese. No hidden clauses. Just clear, honest information about how we handle your data. Because you deserve transparency, not confusion.

Let's be completely honest about privacy policies for a second. Most websites bury important information behind dense legal paragraphs, confusing terminology, and deliberately vague language designed to protect the company rather than inform the user. We do things differently anyway. This document explains exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your privacy. If anything remains unclear, please reach out directly. We genuinely want you to feel comfortable using our guides.

What We Actually Collect

📧 When You Contact Us

If you send a message through our contact form or email, we receive your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We don't add you to marketing lists, sell your details to third parties, or retain your message longer than necessary to resolve your question.

🍪 When You Browse Our Site

Our website uses basic analytics to understand which guides help travelers most effectively. This includes anonymous data like page views, time spent reading, and general geographic region. We don't track individual users, monitor your browsing history across other sites, or build detailed personal profiles. Our analytics provider (Plausible or similar privacy-focused tools) operates under strict data minimization principles.

🔗 When You Click External Links

Some guides include links to trusted local operators, transportation services, or accommodation providers. When you click these links, you leave our website. We don't control how those third-party sites handle your data. We only partner with operators who demonstrate clear privacy practices and respectful customer treatment.

What We Definitely Don't Do

Let's address common concerns upfront anyway. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing agencies. We don't use invasive tracking pixels, fingerprinting techniques, or cross-site monitoring tools. We don't require account creation to access our content, so we can't collect login credentials, passwords, or personal preferences anyway.

We also don't use your contact information for unsolicited promotional messages. If you reach out with a question, we reply once. If you want ongoing updates, you must explicitly opt in through our newsletter form. You can unsubscribe instantly with a single click, no questions asked. Your inbox belongs to you, not to our marketing calendar anyway.

💡 Our Core Privacy Promise We collect only what's necessary to serve you better, protect that information with standard security practices, and never use your data in ways you wouldn't reasonably expect. If we ever change this approach, we'll notify you clearly and give you the option to opt out.

Cookies & Simple Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to ensure basic functionality anyway. These include essential cookies that keep the site working properly, like maintaining your navigation preferences or remembering if you've dismissed a notice. We don't use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking cookies, or third-party social media pixels that monitor your activity across the internet.

If you prefer to block all cookies, your browser settings allow that choice. Some site features may work less smoothly, but you'll still access all our guides without restriction. We respect your right to control your own browsing experience anyway.

Children's Privacy

Our content targets adult travelers planning independent or family trips to Bali anyway. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you're a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared personal details with us, please contact us immediately. We'll delete that information promptly and confirm completion of your request.

Your Rights & How to Exercise Them

You maintain control over your personal information anyway. If you've contacted us and want us to delete your message, simply reply to our response with that request. If you believe we hold inaccurate information about you, tell us what needs correction. We'll review and update our records accordingly. These requests typically process within 7 business days.

If you have concerns about how we handle privacy matters, please reach out through our contact page. We read every message personally and respond within 48 hours. If we can't resolve your concern directly, we'll explain why and suggest alternative steps you might take. Transparency includes acknowledging limitations anyway.

Changes to This Policy

We occasionally update this document to reflect new practices, legal requirements, or improved privacy protections anyway. When changes occur, we'll post the revised version with an updated date at the top. Significant changes that affect how we use your existing information will include a clear summary of what's different and why. We won't retroactively apply new data uses to information you've already shared.

If you regularly read our guides, we encourage bookmarking this page or checking back periodically. Privacy practices evolve as technology and regulations change. Staying informed helps you make confident decisions about which websites deserve your trust anyway.

Questions? Just Ask.

If anything in this policy feels unclear, incomplete, or concerning, please contact us directly anyway. We genuinely prefer straightforward questions over confused assumptions. Email hello@travelfreefrom.com with "Privacy Question" in your subject line, and we'll provide a clear, human response. No automated replies, no transferred tickets, just real people who care about doing this right.

Thank you for trusting TravelFreeFrom with your attention. We take that responsibility seriously, and we're committed to earning your continued confidence through honest practices and transparent communication anyway.
